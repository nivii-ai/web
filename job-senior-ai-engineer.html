Senior AI Engineer

Remote • Full-time

About the Role

As a Senior AI Engineer at Nivii, you'll be at the forefront of building the next generation of business intelligence tools. You'll work on cutting-edge machine learning models and AI systems that help businesses understand their data and make better decisions.

You'll collaborate with our product, engineering, and data science teams to develop sophisticated AI solutions that power our analytics platform. This role offers the opportunity to work on challenging problems in natural language processing, predictive analytics, and automated insights generation.

What You'll Do

  • • Design and implement advanced machine learning models for business analytics and insights generation
  • • Develop and optimize natural language processing systems for query understanding and response generation
  • • Build scalable AI infrastructure and pipelines for model training, deployment, and monitoring
  • • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to integrate AI capabilities into our product platform
  • • Conduct research and experimentation with new AI technologies and methodologies
  • • Mentor junior engineers and contribute to technical architecture decisions

Required Skills

  • • 5+ years of experience in machine learning, AI, or related fields
  • • Strong proficiency in Python and experience with ML frameworks (TensorFlow, PyTorch, scikit-learn)
  • • Experience with natural language processing and large language models
  • • Knowledge of cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, Azure) and MLOps practices
  • • Experience with data processing tools (Pandas, NumPy, Spark)
  • • Strong problem-solving skills and ability to work with large, complex datasets

Nice to Have

  • • Experience with business intelligence or analytics platforms
  • • Knowledge of SQL and database systems
  • • Experience with Docker, Kubernetes, and microservices architecture
  • • Published research or contributions to open-source ML projects
  • • Experience with A/B testing and model evaluation methodologies

Benefits

  • • Competitive salary and equity package
  • • Remote-first work environment with flexible hours
  • • Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance
  • • Professional development budget for conferences and courses
  • • Latest hardware and software tools
  • • Collaborative and innovative team culture

Quick Info
